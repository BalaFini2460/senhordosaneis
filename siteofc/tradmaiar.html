Origins and History

The Maiar were created by Eru Ilúvatar, just like the Valar, before the creation of the world. They participated in the great music of Ilúvatar (Ainulindalë), which shaped the universe.

The Maiar belong to the order of the Ainur but are of lesser power than the Valar.

-When the Valar descended to Middle-earth to shape it, the Maiar accompanied them as helpers and servants.

Characteristics of the Maiar

1- Spiritual Nature:

- They are immortal spirits that can take physical forms but are not bound to them.

- Their appearance varies according to their purpose or will.

2- Skills and Powers:

-The Maiar possess great knowledge and power but vary in strength depending on their role.

-Some have specific abilities, such as control over natural elements or magical skills.

3- Loyalty:

- The Maiar generally serve one of the Valar, acting as extensions of their powers and wills.

- Those corrupted by Morgoth became evil, like Sauron.

Main Group of Maiar

Olórin (Gandalf):

- Maia of wisdom, serving Manwë and Varda.

Sent to Middle-earth as one of the Istari (wizards) to help in the fight against Sauron.

Known for his wisdom, compassion, and ability to inspire courage.
