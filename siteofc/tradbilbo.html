Story

Bilbo Baggins is a 50-year-old Hobbit from the Shire who leads a comfortable and unambitious life, rarely venturing beyond his pantry or wine cellar. But his behavior is disrupted when Gandalf, the wizard, and a company of 13 dwarves knock on his door and take him on an expedition despite calling him a "thief" by Gandalf himself and 13 dwarves led by their king Thorin Oakenshield on a quest to reclaim the Lonely Mountain and its treasure from the Dragon Smaug. The adventure took Bilbo and his companions through the wilderness, to the elven refuge of Rivendell, through the Misty Mountains and Mirkwood Forest, Lake-town in the middle of Long Lake, and eventually to the Mountain itself. Here, after the dragon was slain and the Mountain reclaimed, the Battle of the Five Armies occurred.

On his journey, Bilbo encountered other fantastic creatures, including Trolls, Elves, Giant Spiders, monstrous beings called Stone Giants, a man named Beorn who can shapeshift into a bear, Goblins, Eagles, Wargs, and a viscous, murderous creature called Gollum. Underground, near Gollum's lair, Bilbo accidentally found a magical Ring of invisibility, which he used to escape Gollum.

At the end of the journey, Bilbo became wiser and more confident, having saved the day in many dire situations. He rescued the dwarves from the giant spiders with the magic ring and a short Elven Sword he acquired. He used the ring to hide in hostile environments, as well as his intelligence to free the dwarves from the elves' prisons. He was able to converse with the cunning Smaug. When tensions arose over ownership of the recovered treasure, he unsuccessfully tried to bring the opposing sides to compromise, using a stolen relic as leverage. He had a tense relationship with Thorin, but the two reconciled on Thorin's deathbed. At the end of the story, Bilbo returned to his home in the Shire only to find that several of his relatives, believing him to be dead, were trying to claim his home and belongings. In addition to becoming rich from his share of the dwarves' treasure, he found that he had gained the respectability of experience and wisdom.
